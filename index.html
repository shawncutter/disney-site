The Playbook

6 hacks that change your whole day

These are the moves we actually use — pulled straight from our park trips.

1

The 5-Minute Grace Period

Scan into Lightning Lane up to 5 minutes early — don't wait around for the clock.

2

Mobile-Order on Transit

Order your food from the bus or monorail so it's ready the second you arrive.

3

Park Hop Like a Pro

Chain the monorail, Skyliner, and buses to hit three parks in a single day.

4

Skip the Lightning Lane

Some standby lines move fast — save your passes for the big headliners.

5

The Ride-Breakdown Pivot

Runaway Railroad down? Pivot straight to Star Wars. Make the wait the fun part.

6

Hunt Hidden Gems

Find characters tucked away — like Jasmine in EPCOT's Morocco — and skip the lines.
